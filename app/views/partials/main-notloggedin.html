Welcome to Crowdshipper!

Shop and ship, from Amazon.


Ever wanted to buy something from Amazon,

but was put off by the high shipping costs? Now, you can shop in confidence.

It's simple.

Just browse through Amazon and select what you want, then drop it into a Box.

Pledge a payment with us - as soon as the Box is filled, your payment is processed and the order is placed.

All shipment logistics and orders will be handled by us - delivery to school included!

What if the shipment is delayed?

We cannot guarantee a shipping time. We can, however, provide a weighted average of the shipping times of every single Box that has been shipped through us.
